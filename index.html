Project Overview

Analyzing the challenges, system mechanics, and project scope.

Purpose of FounderLens

FounderLens was developed as an educational demonstration of static data analysis and heuristic auditing. In early-stage startups and small businesses, founders frequently lack the objective resources to identify operational leaks. Retaining strategy consultants is cost-prohibitive, while generic survey templates fail to accommodate industry nuances.

The Problem Solved

This tool processes basic company profiles and models metrics dynamically against historical growth benchmarks. By translating high-level business challenges into structured audits across marketing pipelines, conversion funnels, automation layers, and roadmaps, it demonstrates how raw inputs can be engineered into actionable business intelligence.

Project Scope

  • Static analysis processing
  • Modular state management
  • Dynamic SVG layout building
  • Responsive CSS variables
  • Dual light/dark theme toggles

Project Core Features

Exploring the data modules processed by the evaluation engine.

Business Health Score

Synthesizes overall business alignment into an animated visual score gauge, dividing results into distinct hazard, warnings, and safety zones.

SWOT Matrix Generator

Maps 12 strategic criteria representing core strengths, operational vulnerabilities, market expansions, and external risks based on input parameters.

Marketing Channels Audit

Provides comparative grading across branding, content calendars, lead acquisition, and social channels paired with dynamic advice.

Sales Pipeline Diagnostics

Grades follow-up time delays and checkout conversions, mapping metrics on integrated Chart.js radar charts.

Automation Workflows

Identifies high-frequency manual loops and maps them to API integrators (Zapier, Klaviyo, Sumsub) with dynamic priority markers.

180-Day Timeline Roadmap

Establishes chronological priorities divided into immediate 30-day, medium-term 90-day, and long-term 6-month target objectives.

Methodology Workflow

The processing pipeline behind the heuristic evaluation engine.

01

Enter Details

Input business sector, website URL, stage, team layout, and primary operational friction point.

02

AI Heuristic Analysis

The client-side rules engine parses factors and correlates modifiers across sales and marketing matrices.

03

Assemble Audit Report

Review Insights

Examine performance radar visualizations, progress gauges, and timelines inside the analytics suite.
